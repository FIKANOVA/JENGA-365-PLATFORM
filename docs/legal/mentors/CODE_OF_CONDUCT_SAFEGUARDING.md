# OUTLINE: Jenga365 Mentor Code of Conduct & Safeguarding Policy
*Document Code: LEGAL-MNT-COC-001*

## 1. Professionalism & Integrity
*   Mentors must embody the rugby values: Integrity, Passion, Solidarity, Discipline, and Respect.
*   Zero tolerance for financial exploitation or inappropriate relationship dynamics.

## 2. Safeguarding (Child & Youth Protection)
*   **The "Rule of Three"**: No private, one-on-one virtual or physical meetings without third-party awareness or recorded digital logs.
*   Reporting obligation: Any suspicion of harm to a mentee must be reported to Jenga365 Moderators within 4 hours.

## 3. The "Power Hour" Protocol
*   Mentors commit to 1 high-impact hour per month.
*   Punctuality and respect for the mentee's time are paramount.

## 4. Conflict of Interest
*   Duty to disclose if a mentee is related or if there's a professional overlap that could compromise objectivity.
