# OUTLINE: Jenga365 Mentee Membership Agreement
*Document Code: LEGAL-MNE-AGR-001*

## 1. Membership Obligations (The "Give-Back Clause")
*   Access to free mentorship is contingent upon participation in at least **one (1) impact activity per month**.
*   Commitment to finishing the 6-month cycle.

## 2. The "Three Strikes" Discipline Policy
*   Missing two consecutive sessions without notice result in a "Strike."
*   Three strikes lead to automatic suspension from clinical and networking access.

## 3. Ethical Conduct
*   Respect for mentors' time and professional boundaries.
*   Honesty in self-reporting "Identity Delta" and mood journals.

## 4. Resource Stewardship
*   Agreement to steward educational resources (e.g., books) provided through partner NGO programs.
