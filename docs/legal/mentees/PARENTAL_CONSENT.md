# OUTLINE: Parental / Guardian Consent Form
*Document Code: LEGAL-MNE-PAR-001*

## 1. Minor Status
*   Required for all mentees under the age of 18 (or local legal majority).

## 2. Consent to Mentorship
*   Authorization for the minor to engage with verified professional mentors via the platform.
*   Acknowledgment of safeguarding protocols (Rule of Three).

## 3. Travel & Event Authorization
*   Permission for the minor to participate in local rugby clinics and tree planting activities.

## 4. Emergency Contact
*   Mandatory primary and secondary contact details for the legal guardian.
