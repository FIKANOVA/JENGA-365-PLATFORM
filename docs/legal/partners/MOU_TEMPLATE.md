# OUTLINE: Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)
*Document Code: LEGAL-PRT-MOU-001*

## 1. Purpose of Collaboration
*   Synergizing rugby-based youth mobilization with technical impact execution (e.g., reforestation, education).

## 2. Role of Jenga365
*   Providing the "Muscle": Disciplined, organized volunteer workforce (Mentors & Mentees).
*   Media documentation and Impact Pulse tracking.

## 3. Role of the Partner NGO/CBO
*   Providing the technical expertise, seedlings, or specific welfare resources.

## 4. Resource Exchange
*   Zero-cash exchange model focus: Sharing of human capital for technical resources.
