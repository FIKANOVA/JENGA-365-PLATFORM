# JENGA365 OPERATIONAL MASTER PLAN (2026 - 2027)
**Strategic Theme: Consistency, Growth, and Impact**

## 1. Executive Summary
*(See [EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md](./EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md) for full details)*
Jenga365 is a pioneering dual-engine development initiative positioned at the intersection of high-performance sport and sustainable community development.

## 2. Strategic Foundation
### 2.1 The Problem Statement (The "Athlete's Gap")
1.  **The Identity Crisis**: Athletes struggle with mental well-being outside of their sport.
2.  **The Skills Deficit**: Lack of access to professional networks and career readiness.
3.  **The Environmental & Social Disconnect**: Untapped potential of sports communities for climate action.

### 2.2 Our Vision
To be the global blueprint for the "Total Athlete", creating a world where sports performance, community welfare, and environmental stewardship are built in unison.

### 2.3 Our Mission
To bridge the gap between potential and performance by keeping athletes constructive 365 days a year.

### 2.4 Strategic Objectives (2026–2027)
1.  Institutionalize the "Total Athlete" Model (Q2 2026).
2.  Enhance Socio-Economic Resilience (60%+ secures employment/internships).
3.  Drive Environmental Stewardship (1,000+ trees, 100+ kgs waste by end of 2027).
4.  Foster Mental Well-being (100% access to psycho-social support).

## 3. The Stakeholder Ecosystem
*(See [STAKEHOLDER_GAINS_RETENTION.md](./STAKEHOLDER_GAINS_RETENTION.md) for full details)*
- **Mentees**: Beneficiaries and Agents of Change.
- **Mentors**: Human Capital (The "Power Hour").
- **Partners & NGOs**: Operational Backbone.
- **Corporate Partners**: The Enablers (The "Unlock Challenge").

## 4. The Programmatic Engines
### Engine 1: Human Capital Development (Mentorship)
- **Safe Space Protocol**: NDA-protected discussions on identity and resilience.
- **Digital Matching System**: Algorithm-driven precision matching.
- **Train the Trainer**: Cascading leadership pipeline.

### Engine 2: Social Capital Accumulation (Networking)
- **Webinar Series**: Bi-monthly sessions on financial literacy and branding.
- **Networking Clinics**: "The Boardroom on the Pitch" (bringing corporates to the field).

### Engine 3: Stewardship (Impact)
- **"365" Initiative**: Gamified environmental action (365 trees, 24 photos, etc.).
- **Pass the Knowledge**: Circular economy for educational resources.

## 5. Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability, and Learning (MEAL)
- **Environmental**: Survival Rate vs. Planting Rate (Geotagging & Q-Audits).
- **Social**: Resilience Baselines & Identity Delta (Self-reporting).
- **Economic**: Conversion & Placement Rates (Potential to Pay-check).
- **Learning Loop**: Quarterly reviews and mentee feedback cycles.

## 6. Operational Roadmap
- **Phase 1: Foundation (Q1 2026)**: Digital optimization, "Founding 50" recruitment, Merchandise launch.
- **Phase 2: Launch (Q2 2026)**: CBO Registration, First "Unlock Challenge", Rainy Season tree planting.
- **Phase 3: Expansion (H2 2026)**: Scaling educational reach, Career Shadowing Week.

## 7. Sustainability Strategy
1.  **Financial**: Merchandise, Membership Subscriptions ("Founding 365"), Corporate CSR Grants.
2.  **Operational**: Squad Rotation System (mitigating fatigue), Digital-First Training.
3.  **Environmental**: "Right Tree, Right Place" protocol, GPS Audit Trails.

## 8. Conclusion
Jenga365 is building an institution that proves the discipline of rugby is the exact fuel needed to drive climate action, corporate success, and community welfare.
