# Jenga365 Operational Protocols & Mentorship Model

## 1. The Mentorship Structure
Our mentorship program is designed for maximum efficiency and social ROI, ensuring that professional growth (Total Athlete) directly fuels social impact (ESG).

### 1.1 The "1-to-2" Ratio
*   **Protocol**: 1 Mentor is matched with exactly 2 Mentees.
*   **Rationale**: Distributes the mentor's time effectively while creating a peer-support network between the two mentees.

### 1.2 Timeline & Lifecycle
*   **Cycle Duration**: 6 Months.
*   **Completion Criteria**: Each mentee must finish the structured mentorship curriculum within the 6-month window.

## 2. Impact Requirements
Mentorship is not passive at Jenga365. It is an active engagement with the community.

### 2.1 The Monthly Impact Activity
*   **Mandatory Engagement**: Minimum of **one (1) impact activity per month** per participant (Mentors and Mentees).
*   **Integration**: These activities are leveraged as networking opportunities and "civic education" sessions concerning ESG and Sustainability.
*   **Reciprocity**: Mentors and Mentees participate together, fostering deeper bonds through shared service.

## 3. Scaling & Project Expansion
We aim to scale selected high-impact projects through consistent support:
*   **Pioneer Participation**: Early participants are expected to present and lead different phases of growing projects.
*   **Targeted Support (e.g., Education)**: Ensuring vulnerable children at partner NGOs receive essential resources (e.g., "Books every term") throughout the 6-month session.

## 4. Personnel & Ambassadorship
Through association and active participation, our personnel (manpower) transform into:
*   **Brand Ambassadors**: Promoting awareness for Jenga365 and its corporate/NGO partners.
*   **Technical Resources**: Providing manpower and PR support for partner-led projects.
*   **Talent Pipeline**: Upskilling mentees to the point of being recommended for corporate opportunities and professional mentorship.
